About me:

I am a Junior Research Fellow at Trinity College in Cambridge since October 2023. Before that I did a postdoc with Peter Keevash at the University of Oxford. I completed my PhD with Rob Morris at IMPA in March 2023. My interests have been focused mainly on ramsey theory, random matrix theory, convex geometry, probabilistic and additive combinatorics, but I am open to learning about and working on any nice problems :).
